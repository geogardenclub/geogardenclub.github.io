# Update Crop

<img width="300" src="/img/admin-guide/feature-management/crops/update.png"/>

You can update the crop name, family, and chapter local crop names.

To add a chapter local crop name, choose the chapter from the chapter dropdown, enter the name and click the `Submit` button. 

To remove a chapter local crop name just clear the text field and click the `Submit` button.

You can get to the Delete Crop page by clicking the **Delete** icon in the title bar.
