# Set Gold Varieties

<img width="300" src="/img/admin-guide/feature-management/varieties/gold.png"/>

Gold varieties are the varieties with outcome data from at least three plantings and has achieved a rating of 5 for all five outcome types at least twice.

The `Find Gold Varieties` button will automatically find the gold varieties based on the criteria mentioned above. The `Set Gold Varieties` button will update the gold varieties.
