# Update Price

<img width="300" src="/img/admin-guide/feature-management/prices/update.png"/>

The Update Price page allows you to change the price for a crop and year. Remember that the price field is an integer field that represents the price in cents. For example, if the price is $15.00, enter `1500` in the price field. After making your changes, click the `Submit` button to update the price.

You can indicate that the crop is priceless by clicking the `Is this crop priceless?` checkbox.

Clicking the `Delete` icon will take you to the [Delete Price page](delete).
