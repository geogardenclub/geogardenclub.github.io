# Roadmap

This roadmap provides a high-level view of our goal(s) for each year in the life of GeoGardenClub.

| Year | Project                             | Outcome                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|------|-------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 2021 | Mockup Development                  | Designed and implemented an [executable mockup](/docs/developer-guide/releases/release-0.0/design) to illustrate design innovations.                                                                                                                                                     | 
| 2022 | Mockup Evaluation                   | Evaluated the business concept through [interviews with experienced gardeners](/docs/developer-guide/releases/release-0.0/customer-feedback), and valuate the business concept through [interviews with entrepreneurs](/docs/developer-guide/releases/release-0.0/entrepreneur-feedback). | 
| 2023 | Initial app development             | Built a mobile application that attempts to implement the [Core Value Propositions](/docs/developer-guide/releases/release-1.0/cvp).                                                                                                                                                     |
| 2024 | Release 1.0 (Technology evaluation) | Evaluated the ability of GGC to fulfill its [Technology Goals](/docs/developer-guide/releases/release-1.0/goals).                                                                                                                                                                        |
| 2025 | Release 2.0 (Marketing evaluation)  | Evaluated the ability of the team to implement a  marketing strategy.                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| 2026 | Release 3.0 (Business evaluation)   | (In Process) Evaluate the ability of the team to make progress toward business viability.                                                                                                                                                                                     |
