# Free Preview

:::tip Step 4: Enjoy your Free Preview!
:::

After you register and complete your User Profile, you enter a 7 day "Free Preview" period. This allows you to experience all of the features of GeoGardenClub without agreeing to a subscription.  

You'll know you're in the Free Preview Period through a notification displayed in your Home Screen:

<img width="300" src="/img/user-guide/free-preview.png"/>

At the end of 7 days, the app will ask you to subscribe in order to continue to use it, but all subscriptions come with an introductory offer of three months free. You can cancel anytime during those first three months and pay nothing.
