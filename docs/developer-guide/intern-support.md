# Onboarding

Welcome, new GGC Marketing Intern!  This page provides a checklist of things that may be required to get started for your internship.

## Marketing sites access

Jenna will give you access to the following marketing sites.

* GeoGardenClub: **Wait to create an account until your first meeting with Jenna.**  New users get three months free so you will not need to pay anything during your internship.
* Meta: GGC is active on both Instagram and Facebook.  You will receive full permissions.
* AppStoreConnect: This site tracks downloads and  App Store metrics.  Requires the email associated with your Apple ID.  You will receive "Sales" permissions.
* Apple Ads: This site is where we run ad campaigns for the App Store.  Requires the email associated with your Apple ID.  You will receive "Acount Read Only" access until you have your Apple Ads certificate and preparing to run an ad campaign.
* Google Play: You may or may not receive access to Google Play, since most of our downloads are currently on iOS.
* Google Analytics: This is where we can track metrics related to our website.  A gmail is best for accessing this site.  You will receive "Marketer" permissions.
* Applyra: This site provides ASO support.  Please create your own free account.

## Brand and Audience Discovery

**Website Review**
* What is your first impression (within 10 seconds)?
* What do you think we do and who it’s for?
* What feels clear vs. confusing?
* What actions are you being encouraged to take?

**Social Media Audit (Instagram and Facebook)**
* What themes or topics show up most?
* What tone/voice do we use?
* Which type of posts are most engaging (likes, comments, shares)?
* What feels consistent? What feels scattered?
* Does the content make you want to: Download the app? Follow the account? Learn something about gardening? Unfollow?

**App Store Listing**
* Within 5–10 seconds, what do you think the app does?
* Who does it seem like it’s for?
* What stands out most: Screenshots? Headline/description? Reviews?
* What feels compelling? What feels unclear or missing?
* What story do the screenshots tell?
* Do you want to download the app? Why or why not?

**Competitors**
* Review the website, socials, app store listing for: Seed To Spoon, SeedTime,
Farmer’s Almanac Planner/Grow Veg
* What do they do well in terms of: Onboarding? Community building? Retention?
* How does GeoGardenClub compare?

**Analysis**

Target Audience
* Who do you think our primary audience is?
* What are their: Goals? Pain points? Motivations?
* Are there secondary audiences?

Value Proposition
* What problem are we solving?
* Why would someone choose this over alternatives?
* What feels most compelling? What feels underdeveloped?

Brand Positioning
* How would you describe our brand in 3–5 words?
* How are we different from competitors?

Funnel & Journey
* How does someone go from: Discovering us -> Downloading
* Where are potential drop-off points?

Opportunities
* Where do you think we are: Under-explaining? Overcomplicating? Missing emotional connection?
* What are some quick wins/small changes we could make right away that would increase downloads?

# App Onboarding

* What is the onboarding experience like?  Do the onboarding screens explain the right things?
* Create a garden: Add a planting of something you’d actually like to grow or have growing in your garden
* Explore your chapter through Insights, Observations, Forums, and Index screens
* What feels intuitive vs. unclear?
* When does the app feel most valuable?
* When do you feel most engaged? When do you drop off or feel friction?
* What is the “core value” of the app from your experience?
* Do you feel like our messaging in the App Store/website/socials matches the product?
